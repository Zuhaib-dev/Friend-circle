About Friend Circle

Who We Are

Welcome to Friend Circle — a team of Kashmir-based explorers united by the spirit of adventure and creativity. What started as casual outings among friends has evolved into a journey celebrating trekking, offroading, camping, photography, and teamwork.

Our Passion for Adventure

From the calm of Budgam’s lakes to the rugged terrains of Srinagar and Chadoora, we embrace every adventure. Whether it’s fishing, hiking new trails, or filming our offroading moments, each journey tells a story of courage, nature, and friendship.

Culinary Bonding

Beyond travel, we connect over food. From grilling fish to experimenting with local Kashmiri flavors, our cooking sessions reflect creativity and laughter. We capture these moments in stunning food photography, celebrating our love for exploration on and off the road.
